About Us

We are dedicated to providing relief to flood-affected communities in South Sudan. Our operations are based in Juba, Bor, and Malakal. With your support, we deliver essential aid, rebuild infrastructure, and offer long-term recovery programs.

Our Locations

  • Juba - Main Hub
  • Bor - Relief Distribution Center
  • Malakal - Emergency Response Base

Our Impact

Since our founding, we've helped over 10,000 families with emergency supplies, medical aid, and rebuilding efforts. Join us in making a difference.
